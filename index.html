About us

DigitalTeal is a small team consisting of 4 people, making a new and open-source game called JumpBound.

JumpBound

JumpBound is a realistic 3D platformer with a unique storyline and an air of mystery and adventure. JumpBound allows you to customize your character, collect power-ups, battle enemies, and more!
